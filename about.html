Our Story

From humble beginnings to Buffalo's premier corporate housing provider

2008

Our Founding

716 Corporate Housing was created to solve a simple but critical problem: travel healthcare professionals and corporate employees needed quality, comfortable housing for their temporary assignments in Buffalo.

2010-2015

Building Our Foundation

What started as a small operation with a handful of properties began to grow, establishing partnerships with local healthcare systems and corporations. Our commitment to quality service earned us a reputation for reliability.

2016-2020

Expansion & Growth

We expanded our portfolio across Buffalo's best neighborhoods, serving hundreds of professionals annually. Our success came from our unwavering commitment to quality, service, and making every guest feel at home.

2021-Now

Buffalo's Premier Provider

Today, we manage a diverse portfolio of fully furnished properties across Buffalo, partnering with major healthcare systems, corporations, and academic institutions to provide exceptional housing solutions.

Specialized Housing Solutions

Tailored accommodations for your specific needs

Travel Medical Professionals

We understand the unique needs of medical professionals on the move. That's why our properties are strategically located to offer easy access to major medical centers, ensuring that our guests have a convenient and stress-free stay.

  • Proximity to medical centers
  • Restful recovery environments
  • Flexible scheduling for shift work
  • High-speed internet for telemedicine

Each unit is designed with the comfort and functionality necessary to relax and recharge after a long day, with amenities that cater to both short and long-term stays.

Relocation & Temporary Housing

716 Corporate Housing offers premier housing options for individuals and families relocating to Buffalo or needing temporary accommodations under unexpected circumstances.

  • Fully furnished residences
  • Family-friendly options
  • Flexible leasing terms
  • Convenient Buffalo locations

Whether you're moving to the city, recovering from a home disaster, or visiting family, our fully furnished residences provide a seamless transition with all the comforts of home. Enjoy flexible leasing terms and a variety of properties conveniently located across Buffalo, ensuring comfort and convenience during your stay.
