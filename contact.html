Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to inquiries?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours. For urgent housing needs, we recommend calling us directly at (716) 271-6555 for immediate assistance.

What information should I include in my inquiry?

Please include your desired move-in date, length of stay, number of bedrooms needed, budget range, and any specific requirements (pets, accessibility, location preferences). This helps us provide the most relevant options.

Do you offer virtual property tours?

Yes! We provide virtual tours and detailed photo galleries of all our available properties. We can also schedule video calls to walk you through properties remotely.

What areas of Buffalo do you serve?

We serve the entire Buffalo metropolitan area, with properties in neighborhoods convenient to major hospitals, universities, and business centers. Contact us for specific location availability.
