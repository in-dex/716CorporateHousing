Booking & Reservations

How do I book a property?

Booking is easy! Follow these simple steps:

  1. Browse our Available Units to find properties that match your needs
  2. Text us at (716) 271-6555 with the units you're interested in
  3. We'll send you more details, photos, and arrange a virtual tour if needed
  4. Complete our simple online application form
  5. Sign the lease agreement electronically and pay the security deposit
  6. You're all set! We'll coordinate your move-in details

We look forward to helping you find your perfect home in Buffalo!

What is the minimum lease duration required?

Our properties require a minimum stay of 31 days. We specialize in short to medium-term housing for travel professionals, typically ranging from 1-12 months.

This minimum duration ensures we can provide the high-quality service and amenities that our guests expect, while also being flexible enough to accommodate most travel assignments and temporary relocations.

Can I tour a property before booking?

Absolutely! We offer multiple ways to view our properties:

  • Virtual Tours: Most properties have detailed photo galleries and virtual walkthroughs
  • Video Tours: We can provide live video tours via FaceTime, Zoom, or WhatsApp
  • In-Person Tours: For local guests, we can arrange in-person viewings by appointment
  • Current Availability: Some properties may be occupied, so we'll show you similar available units

Contact us at 716-271-6555 to schedule a viewing.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking as early as possible, especially during peak seasons (May-September). Here's our general guidance:

30+ days
Best availability and rates
14-30 days
Good availability, some popular units may be booked
7-14 days
Limited availability, contact us for last-minute options
0-7 days
Very limited, but we'll try our best to accommodate

Pro Tip: Travel nurses and healthcare professionals should book as soon as assignments are confirmed for the best selection.

High-Speed Internet
Water & Sewer
Electricity
Gas/Heating
Trash Removal
Basic Maintenance

There are no separate utility bills to worry about. Your monthly payment covers everything you need for comfortable living.

Are there any extra fees or hidden costs?

No hidden fees! We believe in transparent pricing. Here's what your monthly payment includes:

Monthly Rent Your all-inclusive housing cost
Security Deposit One-time refundable deposit (typically equal to one month's rent)
No Application Fees We don't charge fees to apply for our properties
No Monthly Service Fees Unlike some corporate housing, we don't add monthly service charges

Optional Services (if needed):

  • Parking (if not included - most properties include at least one parking space)
  • Pet fee (if applicable - typically $25-$50 per month)
  • Additional cleaning services beyond normal turnover

We'll always discuss any additional costs upfront before you book.

Payment Schedule:

Extensions & Changes

What are my renewal options if I want to extend my stay?

We offer flexible renewal options should you wish to extend your stay beyond the initial term. We understand that assignments and plans can change!

Month-to-Month Extension

Continue on a monthly basis after your initial lease term ends

  • No long-term commitment required
  • 30-day notice to vacate
  • Rent may adjust to current market rates

Fixed-Term Renewal

Sign a new lease for a specific period (3, 6, or 12 months)

  • Lock in your rate for the renewal period
  • Priority for unit retention
  • Sometimes includes renewal incentives

Unit Transfer

Move to a different unit if your needs change

  • Switch to larger/smaller unit
  • Change neighborhoods or amenities
  • Minimal transfer fees

Renewal Process:

  1. Notify us at least 30 days before your lease ends
  2. We'll discuss available options and rates
  3. Sign renewal agreement electronically
  4. Continue enjoying your Buffalo home!

Important: Early communication is key! Let us know as soon as you think you might want to extend your stay so we can plan accordingly.

What if I need to leave earlier than planned?

We understand that plans can change. Here's our early departure policy:

Early Termination

If you need to leave before your lease ends

Required: 30-day written notice

Fee: Typically 1-2 months' rent (varies by lease)

Security Deposit: Refunded per normal procedures after unit inspection

Lease Takeover

Find someone to take over your lease

Process: New tenant must apply and be approved

Fee: Reduced or waived early termination fee

Your Responsibility: Help find qualified replacement

Negotiated Agreement

Discuss special circumstances with management

When: Medical emergencies, job relocation, etc.

Outcome: Case-by-case consideration

Documentation: May require supporting documents

Steps for Early Departure:

  1. Contact us immediately to discuss your situation
  2. Submit written notice (email is acceptable)
  3. Discuss available options and associated costs
  4. If applicable, help find a replacement tenant
  5. Schedule move-out inspection
  6. Return keys and complete move-out checklist

Pro Tip: The sooner you communicate with us about potential early departure, the more options we have to help minimize any additional costs.

Location & Buffalo

What should I know about living in Buffalo?

Welcome to Buffalo! Here's what makes our city special:

Four Seasons

Beautiful summers, colorful falls, snowy winters, and blooming springs

Food Scene

Famous for wings, beef on weck, and a growing culinary scene

Niagara Falls

One of the world's natural wonders just 20 minutes away

Arts & Culture

Albright-Knox Art Gallery, Shea's Performing Arts, Buffalo Philharmonic

Getting Around:

  • Public Transit: NFTA buses and Metro Rail
  • Driving: Easy highway access, generally light traffic compared to major cities
  • Biking: Growing network of bike lanes and trails
  • Walkability: Many neighborhoods are highly walkable, especially Elmwood Village and Allentown

Weather Tips:

Winter Bundle up! December-February average 20-30F
Summer Beautiful 70-80F days, perfect for outdoor activities
Lake Effect Can bring sudden weather changes - always check forecasts

Check out our Things to Do in Buffalo page for more local insights and recommendations!
