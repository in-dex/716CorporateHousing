Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

We provide furnishing services across the United States, with a strong presence in major metropolitan areas and popular STR markets. Our nationwide network allows us to efficiently serve clients regardless of location.

How long does the furnishing process take?

Our typical timeline is 3-6 weeks from initial consultation to move-in ready completion. This includes design consultation, furniture sourcing, delivery, and installation. Rush services are available for urgent needs.

Do you offer design services?

Yes! Professional interior design consultation is included in all our packages. We work with you to create spaces that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing, tailored to your target market.

Can you work with my existing furniture?

Absolutely! We can refresh existing spaces by incorporating your current pieces with new items, or we can start completely fresh. Our team will assess what you have and provide recommendations.

What's included in your pricing?

Our quotes include all furniture, accessories, delivery, assembly, installation, and styling. We provide transparent pricing with no hidden fees. Setup fees vary based on property size and scope.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance?

Yes, we offer maintenance and replacement services during guest stays. We can coordinate repairs, replacements, and regular maintenance to ensure your property always looks its best.
